Registration

Please use the Oxford online store to complete your booking. Further information about accommodation and food is also included on the store website.

Registration is open until 28th February 2022.

Abstract submission

Please submit an abstract using this form.

The deadline for submitting abstracts is 28th February 2022, 1700GMT.

Please ensure that you have registered before submitting an abstract. Only abstracts from registered attendees will be considered for talks or posters.

All talks will be recorded and made available to attendees for a period of one month. Please note that by submitting a talk abstract you are consenting to having the talk recorded unless you specifically request otherwise.

Cancellation policy in the event of lockdown

We would like to welcome many of you to Oxford to enjoy the benefits of an in-person meeting. However, we live in uncertain times, and there is a possibility that such in-person meetings will not be possible. In this case, where all attendees will be online, we will endeavour to refund the difference between the payments made and the cost of online attendance. (For example, a student who had paid £350 for the 4 night package would be refunded £225.)
